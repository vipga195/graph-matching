What is SkyDOS?

SkyDOS is a metaverse where people can live the unlimited lives they have always dreamed of. As the name ‘Sky’ implies, this metaverse flies high in the sky and is not controlled from anywhere. ‘DOS’ is for Decentralized Operating Society, which supports people's lives in a decentralized and fair society

This Metaverse is created to enable users to fulfill their dreams and live a richer life. People can access SkyDOS freely, anytime, and from anywhere. In SkyDOS, people can work, play and connect with others.

Through SkyDOS, people can participate in real-world economic activities with users from all over the world.

Avatar Customization

SkyDOS provides a high degree of freedom in avatar customization. It can be linked with various external avatar systems to enable a variety of metaverse experiences, such as Ready Player Me. SkyDOS also plans to provide its own unique NFT avatars as well as support for VRM.
